!ready
	each team must indicate this once to allow draft to start

!startdraft
	once teams are ready, this command will begin the draft timer

!ban name
	team enters the hero they wish to ban

!choose name (name)
	team enters the hero(s) they are picking, seperated by space

!resume
	to be used in case of draft fault with failure to lock pick

!reset
	command will reset all counters and draft status to initial settings.
